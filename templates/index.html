Welcome to TFT Database

Teamfight Tactics is a strategy game that pits you against seven opponents in an auto battler strategy game. Our database will display information about the game such as champions, items, and more. This database will do the following:

Champion Items
This allows users to find what components they need for a specific champion. Users can input what champion they need.
Champion Info
This lists all champions' origins, classes, and cost
Champion Search
Searches for a specific champions and displays the information about them.
Champion Placements
Lists all of the champions and their classes that have a placement better than the one inputted.
Top Champion
Lists the top champion for a specific comp given the origin and class.
Origin Info
Lists all of the origins and their information.
Origin Search
Searches for a specific origin and displays the information about the origin.
Component Search
Finds all the items that have a specific component.
